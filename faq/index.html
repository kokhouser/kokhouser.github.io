So why male models Computer Science?
To be honest, it wasn't ever planned, and I'm quite unsure why I stumbled into CS at all.
Being a mostly-shut-in boy, I naturally gravitated towards computer games. And though it'd be easy to say that I've always wanted to make video games, and that they inspired me to pursue CS, that wouldn't be the truth.
I know a lot of people also get exposed to some flavour of CS earlier on, in high school, but I can't say that I had that privilege, considering the sorry state that the Malaysian public school system is in.
I guess if I were to be totally honest, me ending up almost graduating with a Computer Science degree is probably an extremely unlikely fluke. However, what a beautiful fluke it has been. I can't think of anything else I'd rather be doing in life.
Except of course, alpaca peddling.

How did you go about picking such a horrible domain name?
Well, you see - HEY! It's an extremely clever name, and here's why.
It's obviously a play on Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD), a term thrown about so much for anyone who wants to seem "QuIrKy" and "ohmaigot I'm so OCD", that it has lost all meaning. OCM is meant to be a call-out as well as a meta-joke about how I'm using it wrongly too, also known as a misnomer.
Also, I'd like to think that if OCD is a compulsive drive to repeat obsessive behaviors, OCM would be a drive to repeatedly using the wrong terms and phrases ignorantly. Something I occasionally catch myself being guilty of.
So? Clever huh?
